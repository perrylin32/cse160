One day, I took my flock of ducks to Psyduck's maze, a maze based on their favorite duck! :D
BUT oh no's!!! It would seem like I have lost my all but one of my ducks in this maze! D:
Can you help me find my ducks by traversing through this maze and locating each duck?

Controls:
Up Arrow: Move up
Down Arrow: Move Down
w: Move forward
s: Move backwards
a: Move to the left
d: Move to the right
q: Turn the camera to the left
e: Turn the camera to the right
f: Add a wall to top of stack
r: Delete a wall from top of stack

(P.S: Don't tell anyone, but I heard that this maze contains a literal vault filled with ducks o.o)
